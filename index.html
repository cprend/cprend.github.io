Channing Prend

I am a PhD student studying physical oceanography at Scripps Institution of Oceanography. I use a combination of theory, observations, and remote sensing to investigate large-scale ocean circulation, air-sea interactions, and the role of the ocean in climate. I am particularly interested in Southern Ocean dynamics, including sea ice variability and physical controls on biogeochemistry.

My pursuit of science has always been strongly rooted in a desire to impact society and better our understanding of the changing planet. To this end, I am also very passionate about communicating climate science to the general public. I previously interned at the NYC Mayor's Office of Sustainability helping design public education campaigns on issues related to energy efficiency. I am always looking to participate in outreach activities related to climate change and ocean science, so please contact me with any opportunities.
